The Big Bang Theory

The Big Bang Theory is the leading explanation for how the universe began. It suggests that the universe was once a singularity that expanded rapidly, giving birth to space and time.

Interesting Facts

  • The universe is approximately 13.8 billion years old.
  • Cosmic Microwave Background radiation provides strong evidence of the Big Bang.
  • The universe continues to expand at an accelerating rate due to dark energy.
  • Galaxies are moving away from each other, as observed by redshift in light.
  • The Big Bang was not an explosion but an expansion of space itself.
