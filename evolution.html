Evolution of Earth

The Earth has undergone significant transformations over billions of years, from a fiery ball of rock to the lush and diverse planet we know today.

Key Evolution Stages

  • 4.6 Billion Years Ago - Formation of the Earth from cosmic dust and gas.
  • 4 Billion Years Ago - Cooling of Earth's crust and formation of oceans.
  • 3.5 Billion Years Ago - First simple life forms (microorganisms) appear.
  • 2.4 Billion Years Ago - Great Oxygenation Event due to cyanobacteria photosynthesis.
  • 600 Million Years Ago - Multicellular life begins to evolve.
  • 250 Million Years Ago - Rise of dinosaurs during the Mesozoic Era.
  • 66 Million Years Ago - Mass extinction event, paving the way for mammals.
  • 200,000 Years Ago - Emergence of modern humans.
